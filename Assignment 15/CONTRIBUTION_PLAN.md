## Overall Contribution Strategy
- **Prioritize documentation** → High value, low risk, easier to review.
- **Communicate early** → Commented on issues / PRs where appropriate.
- **Keep PRs small & focused** → One clear purpose per PR.
- **Follow project conventions** → Matched coding/documentation style of each repo.
- **Test thoroughly** → Ensured CI/tests pass before submission.

This plan aligns with open-source best practices and the requirements of Assignment 15.
